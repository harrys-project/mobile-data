Car slams into apartment complex, 1 injured

PHOENIX - A woman was sent to the hospital and a man is in police custody Thursday after the man allegedly drove drunk and crashed his car into an apartment complex, according to the Phoenix Police Department.

No blood-alcohol concentration was not immediately available, but officials say the alcohol levels were likely extreme.

Phoenix police investigators said a man and his wife were returning from a night on the town when the man crashed the car into the gate of the complex where they live, near Hatcher Road and 19th Avenue in Phoenix.

Daniel Brown, who also lives in the apartment complex, said he saw the man trying to reverse the car out of the crash.

“He hopped in the car, tried to back out twice through the hole,” said Brown. “[He] couldn’t back out, so he gunned it around the corner, knocked out a transformer, came over here and parked and just took off.”

Medics took the driver’s wife to the hospital for treatment of unknown injuries.

The driver was taking into custody and faces possible charges aggravated DUI, hit-and-run, and child abandonment.
