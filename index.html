Facebook Instant Articles : a mobile publishing format that enables news publishers to distribute to the Facebook iPhone app. Designed to load 10x faster than a typical article on a mobile device.

    Publishers keep 100% of the ad revenue (our tags are compatible in their layouts.. ex: Tealium, Comscore, Omniture)
    Only available as an iOS option (iPhone)
    RSS type feed (I have an example feed, but we will need to get approved by fb for me to style it EXACTLY as needed)
      All Instant Articles have to be approved by Facebook
      The controls for Instant Articles are on the Tegna Media Facebook page (or in the station pages)
      19 different layout styles to pick from, we import our custom font
    We will need an iPhone 6 to test and debug the pages & articles
