Flight from San Jose to Phoenix makes emergency Los Angeles landing

An American Airlines flight from San Jose, California bound for Phoenix landed in Los Angeles Thursday

An American Airlines flight from San Jose, California bound for Phoenix landed in Los Angeles Thursday after having a mechanical issue.

There were 125 people on board the flight, which landed and arrived at the gate safely, according to American Airlines, which said it was "evaluating" the plane.
